Our Story

Founded in 2020 in Brooklyn, Pretoria, Steel Jab Studio began with one-on-one and small group training sessions. We've since expanded into a premium boxing and martial arts-based fitness studio, targeting high-end clients in Pretoria’s east side.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, clean, and professional fitness environment that combines boxing and martial arts, and strength training tailored to all ages.

Our Vision

We envision becoming Pretoria’s leading boutique boxing and martial arts fitness center known for empowerment, strength, and holistic training and wellness.
